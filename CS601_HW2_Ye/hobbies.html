Video Game

Since I was in college, I was exposed to the game League of Legends because of my roommate. After playing for a while, I fell in love with this game of teamwork and competition.

This game usually requires five teammates to develop the character they choose at the same time, and then win by grabbing some public resources to push down the opponent's base. I have also gained friendship and interest from this game. Although I can't play for a long time due to busy learning tasks, the tens of minutes of game time in my spare time can still make me feel relaxed and happy.

Ski
